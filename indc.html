📊 Technical Analysis Guide: Patterns, Not Magic

🎯 What This Dashboard Is

A real-time BTC analysis tool that fetches live data from Binance. It displays ticks, klines, and 10+ technical indicators to help you identify market patterns and make informed decisions.

  • Live price and volume data
  • RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands
  • Money Flow Index (MFI)
  • Stochastic RSI
  • ATR volatility measure

🧠 How to Use Indicators

Indicators are tools for pattern recognition, not crystal balls. Use them together:

  • RSI > 70 - Possible overbought, watch for reversal
  • RSI < 30 - Possible oversold, watch for bounce
  • MACD crossover - Momentum shift signal
  • BB Width expansion - Volatility increasing
  • Volume confirmation - Validate price moves

📈 Common Patterns

  • Divergence: Price makes lower low, RSI makes higher low → bullish
  • Double Top/Bottom: Look for support/resistance levels
  • Trend lines: Connect highs/lows to identify direction
  • Volume spikes: Often precede major moves
  • Consolidation: Tight ranges often lead to breakouts

⚠️ Important Principles

  • No indicator is 100% accurate
  • Use multiple timeframes (1h, 4h, 1d)
  • Combine with price action analysis
  • Risk management is more important than entries
  • Past performance doesn't guarantee future results
⚠️ DISCLAIMER: This dashboard is for educational and informational purposes only. Technical analysis is pattern recognition, not prediction. Always do your own research and never invest more than you can afford to lose. The indicators shown are tools to help identify potential setups, not guarantees of future price movements.
