The Mission

By early 2024, the crypto-Web3 market was a mix of bold dreams and harsh realities: huge price swings, fragmented protocols, and security breaches made it chaotic. Key points:

  • 2024 Price Volatility: BTC and ETH saw wild rallies and crashes; DeFi and altcoins acted as shock absorbers. The $230M WazirX hack shook confidence.
  • Funding: Q1 2024 brought $2.5B in Web3 funding, mostly speculative projects. By 2025, fewer deals with larger investments reflected selective capital.
  • Structural Weakness: Exploits and bugs cost hundreds of millions. DeFi usage grew, but stability lagged; user engagement remained speculative.
  • Adoption: Wallets and activity increased, but much was trading, airdrops, or meme tokens — not real economic use.
  • 2025 Highlights: Larger capital concentrations, selective funding, and ongoing regulatory scrutiny shaped the market. Security incidents persisted but adoption slowly diversified into NFTs, gaming, and DAO experiments.

Bottom line: Web3 didn't collapse — it matured publicly, shedding illusions and facing real-world complexity head-on.

As a result, our goal is to increase visibility, guide users who don't know where to start, and show how to help strengthen the global economy—because gambling alone will not drive lasting growth.
